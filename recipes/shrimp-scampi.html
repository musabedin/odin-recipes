Shrimp Scampi

Description

Enjoy a quick pasta recipe with an intense garlic aroma and rich spicy butter sauce!

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Pasta
  • Red Chilli Flakes

Steps

  1. Prepare and boil pasta.
  2. Add in butter to a pan. Add in garlic and red chilli flakes. Be careful not to burn.
  3. Add in shrimp, cooking the shrimp until brown on each side.
  4. Once pasta is cooked, drain while keeping some of the starchy water.
  5. Add in pasta into pan with some of the starchy water.
  6. Mix and enjoy.
