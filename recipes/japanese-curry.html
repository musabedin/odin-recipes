Japanese Curry

Description

Enjoy a quick a curry recipe from Japan made easy with an instant pot!

Ingredients

  • Japanese Curry Sauce Mix
  • Chicken/Beef
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Potatoes
  • Chicken Broth

Steps

  1. Prepare and cut choice of meat, and vegetables/potato into bite sized pieces
  2. Use the Saute button on your Instant to preheat and add oil.
  3. Add the sliced onions sweat it. Add the chicken after a couple of minutes
  4. Add the carrots and potatoes and mix well
  5. Add chicken broth and the curry sauce mix.
  6. Pressure cook for 15 minutes
